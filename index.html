How it works

01
Upload your script
Import a Final Draft FDX file for perfect results, or paste your scene text directly. PDF also supported with manual review.
02
Cast the voices
Characters are detected automatically. Assign a profile to each: adult man, young woman, elderly, child — and the app picks the right voice.
03
Listen and refine
Press play. Hear the full cast read your script. Control speed, reading mode and jump between scenes freely.
04
Actor mode
Choose your character. The app reads all other parts and pauses for your lines — perfect for rehearsing and memorizing dialogue.
